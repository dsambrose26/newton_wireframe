- Our Mission -

To create an Alexa Skill for student tutoring purposes.

About

Along with a fully developed front-end website explaining the Alexa Skill developed by us, how its used, its benefits, and future uses for this skill, we are integrating machine learning and artificial intelligence in order to improve on the students academic weaknesses.

In addition, a Slackbot was built to assist in Student's Scheduling. We embedded this functionality into the website and will continue to update many more areas of the app with new technology.


This page will keep you up to date as we build an easy to use Study Tutor over the next few months.
